Google Ads Performance

Looking for ROI? The "Performance Score" metric takes the client's sum of goal values ($1-$10) over the actual cost of the campaigns. This number is pretty small so it is multiplied by 1000 for convenience. For this report to be accurate as possible, ensure GTM tags are configured, GA goals with values are set, and those (or appropriate goals) are imported into Google Ads. More data is available such as CTR and the team members assignments on the PPC Ad Managing Table. This link will take you to the account level details.
